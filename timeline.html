Family, Fights & Everything In Between

At some point, I stopped calling you my friend — not because things got worse, but because that word was no longer enough. You had become my sister. And with that came everything real relationships carry — the warmth, yes, but also the weight. We fought. We hurt each other with words we can't take back. There were silences that felt like walls, days when we stopped talking and I didn't know if we'd find our way back. Those moments broke something in me. Because losing you, even for a little while, was a kind of pain I wasn't ready for. You matter to me more than I ever knew how to say — and I hate that sometimes it took a fight for me to realise it.

🎂 15 May 2026 · Her Birthday

I know we've been through more than just the good times — the fights, the silence, the moments where it felt like things might not find their way back. But here I am, on your birthday, with nothing but honesty in my heart. I am sorry. You became my sister — not just a friend, not just someone I know — my actual sister. And I didn't always treat you the way a sister deserves to be treated. That weight doesn't leave me easily. You have a heart that forgives too quietly and loves too loudly, and I took that for granted more times than I can count. If you can find it somewhere in that big heart of yours to forgive me — truly forgive me — I promise to be the person you always believed I could be. No more fights that end in walls. No more silences that say the wrong things. Just us. Just this. Here's to you, Sister — may this birthday bring you everything you've always given others: warmth, love, and the feeling of truly being seen. 🎉✨
